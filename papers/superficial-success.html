Superficial Success vs. Internal Breakdown: An Empirical Study of Generalization in Adaptive Multi-Agent Systems

ACL 2026 Findings
Namyeong So, Seokgyu Jang, Taeuk Kim

One-Line Summary

An empirical study analyzing cases where models appear to perform well on the surface but fail internally

Key Points

  • Systematically analyzes the phenomenon where models achieve seemingly high performance while their internal mechanisms do not function correctly
  • Diagnoses the gap between superficial success and internal breakdown, and proposes more reliable evaluation methodologies
