Hyper-CL: Conditioning Sentence Representations with Hypernetworks

ACL 2024
Young Hyun Yoo, Jii Cha, Changhyeon Kim, Taeuk Kim

One-Line Summary

A method for conditionally generating sentence representations via hypernetwork-based contrastive learning

Key Points

  • A contrastive learning framework that leverages hypernetworks to flexibly generate sentence representations conditioned on tasks or criteria
  • Experimentally validates the effectiveness of conditional sentence embeddings across various downstream tasks
