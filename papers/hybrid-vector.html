Hybrid-Vector Retrieval for Visually Rich Documents: Combining Single-Vector Efficiency and Multi-Vector Accuracy

ACL 2026 Findings
Juyeon Kim, Geon Lee, Dongwon Choi, Taeuk Kim, Kijung Shin

One-Line Summary

A hybrid vector retrieval approach for visually rich documents combining text and visual information

Key Points

  • Improves document retrieval performance through hybrid vector representations that combine textual and visual information
  • Presents effective retrieval strategies for documents rich in visual elements such as layouts, tables, and figures
