Element-wise Bilinear Interaction for Sentence Matching

*SEM 2018
Jihun Choi, Taeuk Kim, Sang-goo Lee

One-Line Summary

A sentence matching method using element-wise bilinear interaction

Key Points

  • Performs fine-grained semantic matching by modeling element-wise bilinear interactions between sentence pairs
  • Improves sentence matching task performance through a concise yet effective interaction mechanism
