Aligning Language Models to Explicitly Handle Ambiguity

EMNLP 2024
Hyuhng Joon Kim, Youna Kim, Cheonbok Park, Junyeob Kim, Choonghyun Park, Kang Min Yoo, Sang-goo Lee, Taeuk Kim

One-Line Summary

A method for aligning language models to explicitly handle ambiguity in questions

Key Points

  • Proposes techniques for aligning language models to recognize and explicitly handle input ambiguity
  • Improves the ability to present multiple possible interpretations or request clarification for ambiguous questions
