BlendX: Complex Multi-Intent Detection with Blended Patterns

LREC-COLING 2024
Yejin Yoon, Jungyeon Lee, Kangsan Kim, Chanhee Park, Taeuk Kim

One-Line Summary

A method for detecting multiple intents from a single utterance in a complex manner

Key Points

  • Proposes a complex multi-intent detection framework that simultaneously recognizes multiple intents within a single utterance
  • Effectively handles complex utterances with mixed multiple intents in real conversational environments
