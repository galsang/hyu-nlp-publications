When to Speak, When to Abstain: Contrastive Decoding with Abstention

ACL 2025
Hyuhng Joon Kim, Youna Kim, Sang-goo Lee, Taeuk Kim

One-Line Summary

A study on when LLMs should answer and when they should abstain in uncertain situations

Key Points

  • Establishes criteria for LLMs to appropriately abstain instead of generating uncertain answers
  • Proposes a selective response strategy to improve the reliability and accuracy of generated answers
