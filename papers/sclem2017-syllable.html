A Syllable-based Technique for Word Embeddings of Korean Words

The 1st Workshop on Subword and Character Level Models in NLP (SCLeM 2017) at EMNLP 2017
Sanghyuk Choi, Taeuk Kim, Jinseok Seol, Sang-goo Lee

Summary

This paper proposes a syllable-based technique for generating word embeddings of Korean words, leveraging the unique syllabic structure of the Korean writing system (Hangul).
