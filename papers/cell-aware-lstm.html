Cell-aware Stacked LSTMs for Modeling Sentences

ACML 2019
Jihun Choi, Taeuk Kim, Sang-goo Lee

One-Line Summary

Improving sentence modeling with cell-aware stacked LSTMs

Key Points

  • Proposes a mechanism for effectively propagating cell state information across layers in stacked LSTM architectures
  • Enhances sentence semantic representation through cell state awareness and improves performance on various NLP tasks
