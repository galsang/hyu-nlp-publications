Ground-Truth Labels Matter: A Deeper Look into Input-Label Demonstrations

EMNLP 2022
Kang Min Yoo, Junyeob Kim, Hyuhng Joon Kim, Hyunsoo Cho, Hwiyeol Jo, Sang-Woo Lee, Sang-goo Lee, Taeuk Kim

One-Line Summary

An analysis showing that correct labels play a crucial role in in-context learning

Key Points

  • Provides an in-depth analysis of how ground-truth labels in demonstration examples affect model performance during in-context learning
  • Identifies the conditions and mechanisms under which models learn more effectively when provided with correct input-label mappings
